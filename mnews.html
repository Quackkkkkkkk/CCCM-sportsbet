Holy Cross Ryde Defeat Marcellin to go Undefeated This Season
This Thursday, Holy Cross College Ryde defeated Marcellin College Randwick in all of their Junior, Intermediate, and Senior Touch Football Games. This means that Holy Cross have now have won every single Touch Football Game they have played in every Age Group this Season, becoming the first school to achieve such a feat 3 years in a row.
